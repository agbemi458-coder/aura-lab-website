The Science of Skin

Learn more about the powerhouse ingredients our AI may recommend.

Hyaluronic Acid

A hydration superstar, this molecule can hold up to 1000 times its weight in water. It draws moisture into the skin, providing a plump, dewy look that's beneficial for all skin types.

Vitamin C

A potent antioxidant that protects skin from environmental stressors. It's renowned for its ability to brighten the complexion, fade dark spots, and boost collagen production for firmer skin.

Niacinamide

Also known as Vitamin B3, this versatile ingredient is a true multi-tasker. It helps minimize the appearance of pores, controls oil, soothes redness, and improves the skin's barrier function.
